About the organization

Student leadership, coordination, and communication

The EASTC Students Organization supports student welfare by coordinating academic, social, sports, cultural, leadership, and community activities. It also helps students receive official announcements and raise important concerns through organized representation.

Coordination

Schedules activities, student meetings, club sessions, and campus events.
